About Us

We encourage you to visit our website and discover the reason our company has the ability to give you what you need -- your cargo delivered safe and sound. Company Information

TWT is an independently family-owned transportation business. We have been in the transportation business since the early 50's, when Charles Monin hauled his first load of bulk milk. Through hard work and dedication, we have grown from a single truck operation to over 50 trucks. Today, our fleet consists of 65-70 tractors, over 300 trailers, 35 are refrigerated units. It was never our goal to become a big trucking company but to provide excellent service based on the needs of our customers, we grew. Being from the bourbon capital of the world, our primary focus has become the bourbon industry. We have had the pleasure of working within the automotive industry, as well as working with some of the largest distribution centers in the U.S.. Our sister company, Bardstown Warehousing, offers solutions for your warehousing and distribution needs.
